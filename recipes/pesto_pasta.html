Pesto Pasta

Description

A quick 15 minute recipe that's a favourite for all: Pesto Pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta - shape of your choice
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Pesto - Home-made or store bought
  • Cheese

Preparation and Cooking

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain
  2. Cook the onion in the oil, then stir in pesto and any seasoning
    3. ]
  3. Add pesto-seasoning-onion mixture to your hot pasta and add any cheese
